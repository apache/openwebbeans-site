Coordinates:

<dependency>
  <groupId>org.apache.meecrowave</groupId>
  <artifactId>meecrowave-jta</artifactId>
  <version>${meecrowave.version}</version>
</dependency>

This allows to use @Transactional and @TransactionScoped features of JTA 1.2.
